About Me

Hello, my name is James Ravelle! Thank you for visiting my website. I am currently enrolled in the Penn LPS Boot Camp in Philadelphia, Pa. I also have an MBA and BA in Marketing from Moravian College in Bethlehem, PA.

I started my career path working for the Lehigh Valley Arts Council managing their website and marketing campaigns. Next, I worked as a graphic designer and print specialist for commercial printer Bethlehem Business Forms / Working Dog Press. After this I went on to do website administration, graphic design and email/print campaign management for the marketing agency Marketing Partners. For the past several years I have worked as a Digital Marketing Specialist for special education staffing company Therapy Source Inc. Additionally, over the past few years I have worked as a freelance website developer, designing and coding several custom WordPress websites. I also freelance as a graphic designer, illustrator and animator - art has been a passion of mine for most of my life.
